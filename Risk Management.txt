RISK MANAGEMENT PLAN

Identified Risks:
Delay in task completion
Probability: Medium
Impact: High
Mitigation: Assign deadlines and monitor progress in Trello
Lack of communication
Probability: Medium
Impact: High
Mitigation: Regular team updates and meetings
File loss or version confusion
Probability: Low
Impact: High
Mitigation: Use GitHub version control and commits
Unequal workload distribution
Probability: Medium
Impact: Medium
Mitigation: Assign clear responsibilities to each member
